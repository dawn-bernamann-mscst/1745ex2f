Smokin Hot Jerseys, Holsteins and Ayrshires is focused on breeding high quality registered animals that compete at the highlest levels of competition. Our goal is to develop families with high-scoring individuals and multiple generations of All-American nominations.

Smokin' Hot is owned by Austin Nauman and his mother Danielle. Located in western Wisconsin, the herd is limited to an elite group of individuals who have been selected for the depth of their cow families.
